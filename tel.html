HEART RESCUE

The solution deployed here is to enable the rapid detection of 8 majors groups of heart failure :

  • Rythms
  • Electric axis of the heart
  • Conduction abnormalitie
  • Extrasystolie
  • Hypertrophie
  • Cardiac pacing
  • Ischemia
  • Non-specific repolarisation abnormalies

NB: For better accuracy, the electrical signal transmitted must be sampled at300 Hz along the six frontal leads and the six horizontal leads
